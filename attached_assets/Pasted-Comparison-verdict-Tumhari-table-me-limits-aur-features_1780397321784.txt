Comparison verdict
Tumhari table me limits aur features ka flow natural lag raha hai:

usage metrics gradually grow kar rahe hain,

advanced workflow features mid-tier se unlock ho rahe hain,

governance and analytics upper tiers me ja rahe hain,

AI quality and volume bhi proper ladder follow kar raha hai.

Ye achha sign hai because packaging me sabse important hota hai ki customer ko har next plan ka reason clearly samajh aaye. Tumhari current structure me woh reason mostly clear hai.

Kya bilkul sahi hai
1. Usage ladder
Workspaces, seats, signals, accounts, active problems ka progression sensible hai: 1 → 1 → 3 → unlimited ya phir modest → medium → serious → large-scale. Yeh value-aligned lagta hai aur product adoption ke saath naturally grow karta hai.

2. Launches ladder
Free me 1 active / 3 lifetime, Starter me 5 active, Growth me 15 active, Scale me unlimited — yeh bahut strong decision hai. Isse har paid tier ka clear operational ceiling banta hai, aur lower tiers higher plans ko cannibalize nahi karte.

3. Opportunities and workflow unlocks
Top 5 only → full list → saved views → custom score views/export ka progression bahut acha hai. Isse user pehle insight dekhta hai, fir workflow run karta hai, fir analysis aur governance unlock karta hai.

4. AI packaging
~100 → ~1,500 → ~4,000 → ~10,000 AI calls plus Standard → Higher-quality → Top-tier model framing bhi kaafi clean hai. AI products me usage + quality dono ka ladder meaningful hota hai, aur tumne woh sahi pakda hai.

Kya thoda improve karna chahiye
1. “Completed Launches: Unlimited” Starter se theek hai
Isko change mat karo. Active launches hi real constraint hai; completed launches ko restrict karna often low-value friction hota hai. Current setup better hai.

2. “Accountability Dashboard” row ko aur clear banao
Abhi “Basic / Full / Exec + Board view” thoda abstract lag sakta hai. Isko rename karo:

Free: Basic launch status widgets

Starter: Launch & verdict widgets

Growth: Outcome analytics (ARR, segments, verdicts)

Scale: Executive & board reporting view

This makes the value clearer and packaging more transparent. Transparent packaging is a core pricing best practice.

3. “Decision Memo & PRD Drafts: 1× per decision / Bulk generation” ko cleaner karo
Abhi row slightly uneven lag rahi hai, because free me entitlement phrasing hai aur higher tiers me workflow phrasing. Better:

Free: 1 draft per decision

Starter: Standard drafts

Growth: Bulk drafts

Scale: Bulk drafts + premium models

Isse comparison easier ho jayega.

4. “Activity Log” progression sharpen karo
Abhi Basic → Advanced + Filters → Audit Trail Export → Granular Roles thoda mix lag raha hai because roles and activity log same category me nahi hain. Isko do categories me split karo:

Governance

Activity Log

Audit Trail Export

Granular Roles

Support & Success

Support Level

Onboarding Session

Weekly Summary Email

Quarterly Check-in

Ye presentation better hoga.

Jo rows add karni chahiye

Product Areas / Segments — if unlimited across all, mention “Included” or leave out entirely.

Notifications & Reminders — Day 7/30 reminders all plans me hain ya nahi, clear karo.
