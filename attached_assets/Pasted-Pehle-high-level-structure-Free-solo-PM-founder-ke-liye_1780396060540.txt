Pehle high‑level structure
Free → solo PM / founder ke liye sandbox + proof of value.

Starter → chhota team (2–3 makers) ke liye proper weekly use + limited launches.

Growth → serious PM org jahan launches & outcomes core ritual hain.

Scale → multi‑squad org jahan governance, analytics, aur high AI volume chahiye.

Is hisaab se features split karte hain.

Free plan – kya dena hai (intentionally limited)
Goal: banda 1–2 cycles complete kare (Signals → Problem → Opportunity → Decision → Launch → Verdict), lekin scale pe use karne ke liye forced ho jaye upgrade.

Extra: Free vs Paid hooks — kya purposely lock karna chahiye
Jitni cheezein neeche hain, inko Free me ya to bilkul mat do, ya sirf taste do:

Compare Opportunities: sirf paid (Starter+) me.

More than 1 active launch: paywall.

Advanced analytics / dashboard breakdowns: Growth+.

Multiple workspaces: Growth+.

High AI usage & premium models: Growth/Scale only.

Activity log / governance features: Growth+.

Isse flow yeh banega:

Free user ko complete story milti hai, par ek hi bet / chhota volume.

Jaise hi woh real team workflow shift karta hai, woh automatically Starter barrier hit karega.

Growth/Scale decision usually leadership karegi jab woh dekhe ki:
“ye tool se hum quarterly bets and outcomes board tak le ja sakte hain.”
